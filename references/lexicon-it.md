# Italian corporate lexicon (and office itanglese)

Mappings from plain language to Italian business register. The "corporate" column
rises in intensity from left to right. In Italian, real corporate speak is often
mixed with English (itanglese): use it, it is authentic. The dose of English grows
with the level.

## Dosage notes per level

- **Level 1:** almost always pick the soberest Italian option in the corporate
  column; minimal itanglese (only what is now effectively Italian: call, mail,
  budget, team). No "leveraggiare", no "closare".
- **Level 2:** natural Italian/English office mix; allineamenti, deliverable, KPI,
  stakeholder, quick win.
- **Level 3:** maximum density, heavy itanglese, bridge phrases stacked, but always
  pass the anti-AI filter (no mechanical rule of three, no em dashes, operational
  closings).
